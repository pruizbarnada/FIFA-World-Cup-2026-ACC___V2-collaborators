3rd Place Order
Out of the 12 countries classified 3rd in the group stage, only the best 8 will classify to the knockout rounds. Which countries do you think these will be?

Rank the 8 third-place teams you think will advance (most group-stage points = rank 1). Use ▲ ▼ to reorder. Pick all 12 third-place teams in the Groups tab first.
Knockout Bracket
Tap a team to pick the match winner, and enter your predicted score to win bonus points.
Phase two unlocks the full knockout bracket at once. Each match still locks 30 minutes before kickoff.
Note: the score is the final result after penalties - if a match is decided in the penalty shoot-out, give the winning team the higher number of goals.
Scoring System
Points are only awarded once real results are recorded by the admin. Filling in your bracket alone scores 0.
